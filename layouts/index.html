Exploring the intersection of neuroscience, genomics, and public understanding requires both rigorous research and effective communication. My work combines computational approaches to understand the genomic basis of brain development with efforts to translate these findings for broader audiences.

This website is my space to share both my scientific research and my perspectives on science communication. I believe that making complex science accessible is not just about simplification—it's about creating meaningful connections to people's lives and experiences.
